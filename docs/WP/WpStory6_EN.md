# II. The new branch — Three Streams of One River

## 1. What the tree has done all along

Since the first block, one simple rule has held: whoever wants ZION must
mine it, or receive it from someone who did. A miner points GPU or CPU at
the network, the machine solves the Ekam Deeksha equation, the network
verifies the work, and the miner is rewarded directly in ZION.

That rule does not change. It is the root, and the root does not move.

## 2. What's new in v3.0.6

Starting with **v3.0.6-beta**, the official ZION miner carries a new
capability: **Triple Stream**.

> **Mine ZION. Earn ZION. Grow ZION.**

Triple Stream is a smart mining engine built directly into the official
ZION miner. It turns on automatically — no configuration, no second wallet,
no second application needed. Its job is one thing: **extract more value
from your hardware than the Ekam Deeksha core alone could, and pay all of
that value out to you in ZION.**

In plain terms: your machine (GPU and CPU) works at full capacity, the
network internally distributes the work so no cycle goes unused, and the
result lands in your wallet as a single line item — **ZION, growing.**

Exactly how the engine distributes work between GPU and CPU is part of the
proprietary architecture of the ZION miner (much like many optimized mining
cores in other networks keep their internals closed). The ZION core and
pool source code remains fully open under the MIT license — the Triple
Stream engine is a bonus layer on top of it, available for free to anyone
running the official ZION miner.

## 3. Zion Grow — a position that keeps growing

**Zion Grow** is the name for what happens to your wallet when you mine
with the Triple Stream engine:

- Every block your hardware contributes to increases your ZION balance.
- You never need to sell, swap, or watch an exchange.
- The longer you mine, the more ZION you hold — the position compounds
  simply by leaving the miner running.

There is no profit promise in this. It is a simple mechanism: the work your
hardware performs is fully converted into your balance in a single
currency, instead of five different tokens you would otherwise have to
sell yourself.

## 4. Zion Liquidity — why this strengthens the whole network

Most mining networks share the same problem: a miner mines a coin, sells it
on an exchange to pay for electricity, and that pushes the price down. The
more people mine, the greater the sell pressure.

**Zion Liquidity** inverts this pattern:

- The Triple Stream engine internally converts all mined value into ZION
  before anything ever reaches an exchange.
- The miner never needs to sell anything — they receive ZION directly.
- The result is that mining **creates no sell pressure** on the ZION price.
  On the contrary — with every additional hash, the liquidity depth the
  network draws from grows deeper.

This is the reverse of the old mining pattern described in the Quantum
Revolution chapter of the main whitepaper: the river that used to flow
uphill now flows downhill, toward those who do the work.

## 5. Why this is a bonus, not a requirement

Triple Stream requires nothing extra. It is not a new fee, not a new
registration, not something you need to configure. It is a property of the
official ZION miner v3.0.6 and later — if you mine with it, you receive the
bonus automatically. If you use an older version or your own setup, you
still mine under the original, fully open Ekam Deeksha rule — just without
this acceleration.
