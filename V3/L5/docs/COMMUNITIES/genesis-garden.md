## 1. Identity and Purpose

Genesis Garden is the **first physical node** of the Terra Nova network in Europe — an eco-farm where organic agriculture, surf, off-grid technology, and community life intersect. It serves as the **Base Camp**: a place where Guardians and supporters come for retreats, work stays, and educational programs, and where everyone can experience real off-grid operation.

The intent is not perfection — the intent is **real operation open to people**. A farm that grows alongside humans, not without them.

> *"One love, one heart. Together we create a future where humanity and Earth are again in harmony."*

## 2. Legal Structure

| Aspect | Status | Details |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **Legal entity** | 🟡 In preparation | Cooperative (Cooperativa de Habitação e Agricultura) or Portuguese non-profit (Associação) |
| **Land ownership** | 🟡 Negotiating | Target: 2–5 hectares with agricultural water rights |
| **Zoning** | 🟡 Researching | Must permit: agriculture, eco-tourism, temporary structures |
| **Building permits** | 🔵 Not yet required | Phase 1 uses temporary/glamping structures |
| **Tax residency** | 🔵 TBD | Portuguese tax optimization for non-profit agricultural entities |

### Recommended legal path

1. **Phase 0–1:** `Associação` (non-profit association) — lowest barrier, fast registration, no minimum capital
2. **Phase 2+:** Transition to `Cooperativa` (cooperative) — allows profit-sharing, member equity, agricultural subsidies
3. **Alternative:** `Holding structure` — Dutch Stichting or Estonian OÜ as parent, Portuguese subsidiary as operator (for international funding)

## 6. Governance

### 6.1 Off-chain: Sociocratic Circles

```
General Circle (all Guardians + long-stay members)
    ├── Operations Circle (daily farm, guests, maintenance)
    │       ├── Agriculture Sub-circle
    │       ├── Hospitality Sub-circle
    │       └── Infrastructure Sub-circle
    ├── Finance Circle (budget, accounting, ZION node)
    ├── Community Circle (conflict, wellness, culture)
    └── Expansion Circle (new projects, partnerships, L5 network)
```

**Decision-making:** Consent-based (not consensus — "no reasoned objection" threshold). Double-linking: each sub-circle elects a representative to parent circle.

### 6.2 On-chain: ZION DAO

| Function | Mechanism | Threshold |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Treasury spending (< 500 EUR) | Operations wallet, 2-of-3 | Immediate |
| Treasury spending (500–5,000 EUR) | Finance Circle proposal, 3-of-5 | 48h review |
| Treasury spending (> 5,000 EUR) | General Circle vote → on-chain proposal | 7 days, 60% majority |
| New Guardian admission | Community Circle → General Circle → DAO record | 14 days, consent |
| Protocol upgrades (node, mesh) | Tech lead + Finance Circle | 48h review |

### 6.3 Guardian Roles

| Role | Responsibility | Minimum commitment |
